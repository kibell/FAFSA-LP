<<Preferred>>, Here’s how you can minimize delays in getting your financial aid offer

Dear <<Preferred >>,

We're thrilled that you're applying to SNHU and can't wait for you to join us! Our next available term start date is May 6.



As you may know, there are nationwide delays to federal financial aid processing this year. If you’re interested in using federal financial aid, here are the things you can do now to minimize delays to receiving your financial aid offer and registering at SNHU:

1. Complete the 2024-25 Free Application for Financial Aid (FASFA)

If you’re planning to use federal financial aid, you need to complete this form. Due to the delays and maintenance issues with the FAFSA website this year, it's important to complete your FAFSA as soon as possible.

SNHU’s FAFSA school code is 002580.

If you have questions or need help filling out the FAFSA, call SNHU Student Financial Services (SFS) at 877.455.7648 or to schedule a call.

2. Call us to confirm you’ve completed your FAFSA

By calling your SNHU admission counselor at 888.387.0864 or emailing them at admissions@snhu.edu, you’ll will alert us to be on the lookout for your FAFSA info, so we can create your financial aid offer.

3. Call us to confirm we have your Social Security number (SSN)

While your SSN is not required for admission to the university, we cannot review your FAFSA or create a financial aid offer without it. Call your SNHU admission counselor at 888.387.0864 to ensure we have your SSN on file.

Completing the steps above will help us create your financial aid offer and reach out to with next steps.

If you have any questions, please don’t hesitate to reach out to your admission counselor at 888.387.0864.
